Steps for Preparing a Cup of Tea (Ordered List)

  1. Boil water.
  2. Place a tea bag in a cup.
  3. Pour the hot water into the cup.
  4. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Remove the tea bag and add sugar or milk if desired.
  6. Stir well and enjoy your tea.
